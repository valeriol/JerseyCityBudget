As we await data for the FY 2014 Introduced Budget, we've moved to scrape the historical data from the Jersey City Offical Documents page.

The historical data scraping involves liberating the data from PDFs. We will start the process during our Hack Night on January 7th, 2014 and continue during the National PDF Liberation Event on January 17-19, 2014. The liberated data will be uploaded into our open data portal.

Visualization tools will be created to help explain the budget.

We would love to hear from you -- commmunity advocates, coders, journalists.
